About Me

Starting at the young age of 5, Bridget was introduced into the creative world of dance. Ever since then she has delved into multiple creative feilds including: webpage design, musical theater, clarinet, choreography, cooking, drawing, acting, writing, and crafts. She is excited about her new endeavor into coding and looks forward to what the future will bring with the new skills.

She can frequently be found on the corner of Carnegie and Ontario cheering on her Cleveland Indians as a season ticket holder. An avid football fan, she has been a die hard through all the ups and (mostly) downs of her beloved Cleveland Browns and is excited for next season (but for real this time!).
