About Me

She can frequently be found on the corner of Carnegie and Ontario cheering on her Cleveland Indians as a season ticket holder. An avid football fan, she has been a die hard through all the ups and (mostly) downs of her beloved Cleveland Browns and is excited for next year (but for real this time!).
